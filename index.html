Who are the Circuit Sorcerers?

A new FRC team with a big vision and a bigger work ethic.

We are an enthusiastic community of students and alumni coming together to create a new FIRST Robotics Competition (FRC) team, eager to explore the realm of STEM through hands-on learning. Our mission is to give members the chance to build real-world engineering, programming, design, and teamwork skills, preparing us not only for competition, but for our futures.

What sets us apart is our creative drive. We aim to inject a powerful visual presence into everything we do, from our robot to our presence at competitions. Since we are a rookie team starting in the 2026 robotics season, we do not have a long list of achievements yet. That does not mean we are not going to try and give it our all.
